Hands-on Learner

Embarking on projects are chiefly the fastest way to pick up new skills and interests, and I am always getting my hands dirty in search for new opportunities to learn.
My stint with a sustainable cities research team ushered me into the world of Android development and wireless communications, where I got to interface an environment sensor through a smartphone. I spent the next summer at a senior care startup learning the dynamics of working in a software engineering team, sharpening my app development skills in the process. My desire to learn and experience new things does not end upon joining your start-up—I am looking forward to working together with your team in whichever area required.

Collaborator and Communicator

Every day, I have the privilege of interacting with a diverse mix of faculty and students in the National University of Singapore (NUS) University Scholars Program. There are ample opportunities to work on projects together but the challenge I have learned to overcome is to present a point of view to someone from a different background and perspective.
Furthermore, in my capacity as Marketing Director of the NUS Entrepreneurship Society, I was given the opportunity to lead a team of marketers, web developers, and designers. Our various projects include revamping our website and revitalizing content marketing initiatives, which I gained invaluable exposure to. The process of designing a website trained me to pay attention to the user experience and taught me how to build products that people will enjoy and use.


Why Start-up?

Having successfully completed two internships at start-ups in Singapore, I gravitate towards the start-up for its awesome culture, high-speed pace (and therefore growth), and genius ideas that nobody ever thought of.
I want to tackle problems our communities take for granted with technology for a living, and am willing to grow as a software engineer through team collaboration, consistent review, and self-learning new languages and technologies.


Hobbies and Interests

“Sometimes the most scenic roads in life are the detours you didn't mean to take.” Road trips to nowhere resonate with me, as well as meeting people and experiencing new cultures. I jam on the piano with a few local bands and make short films in my free time.
